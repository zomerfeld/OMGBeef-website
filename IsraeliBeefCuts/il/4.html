כתף מרכזי - Katef, Katef Mercazi

American
Arm Roast, Shoulder Roast

This cut is low on fat, has a smooth texture and a thin connecting tissue in the middle (almost unnoticeable). It can be used for a pot roast, or cut up for stew meat. A steak cut from this roast is called an arm steak.
